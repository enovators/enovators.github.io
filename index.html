Frequently asked questions

If you cannot find an answer here please do not hesitiate to Contact Us

How long will it take for my case to arrive?

Short Answer : 8 - 12 Business Days.
Long Answer- After you place your order, one of our representatives will contact you with a digital mockup of your case within two business days. After your approval, the laser engraving process will take upto an additional three business days. USPS shipping to the continental United States can take anywhere from 5-7 business days.

What phones do you currently sell cases for?

We have cases readily available in stock for the iPhone 6/6s. But fear not, you can still order cases for other phones. For those phones it takes an additional five business days as the case has to be shipped to us first.

What kind of cases do you offer?

We currently only offer clear cases. However, If you want a specific case laser engraved, you can send it to us and we will happily engrave it for you.

What is your return policy?

Since all items we sell are made to custom orders, we do not offer returns for buyer's remorse. In the case that there is a defect, please Contact Us.

How secure is my information?

All of our payment is processed and encrpted by Paypal so we never get to see your payment information. Additionally, we will never sell or distribute your information.

How can I place an order?

Please navigate to this page to start the order process. The form will ask for the design and the phone that you need the case for. After you place an order, one of our representatives will contact you.
